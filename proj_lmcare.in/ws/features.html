L-MCARE Application Features and Plans

Appointment

  • Auto appointment by patients.
  • Configuration of appointment slots by doctors.
  • Reschedule/Cancel/Swap appointment by sending message.
  • Appointment Alerts on your APP.

 

Clinic Management

  • Doctor register user’s (Patients, Pharmacy and Diagnostic Labs)
  • All registered user’s issued free mobile app.
  • All registered users can access their related information any time anywhere from APP or PC.
  • Patient APP view – Prescription Details and Diagnostic Details.
  • Pharmacy APP views – Patient Prescription details.
  • Diagnostic lab APP view – Patient lab test information.

 

E-Health File

  • Patient assessment updated as long as patient visits clinic, can view patient history with prescription details.
  • A common E-Health file maintained for registered users, upon visiting any clinic doctor can update file after diagnosis.

 

E-Prescription

  • Patient as well as Pharmacy shops view prescribed medicine details through their mobile APP.
  • Alternative medicine request sent to doctor in case of prescribed medicine not available.
  • Pharmacy Shops can share the medicine database to doctor for accurate prescription to patients.

 

E-Diagnostic View

  • Patient as well as diagnostic labs views prescribed tests through their mobile APP or PC.
  • Diagnostic labs can directly enter the parameters in pre drafted test templates in patients file.
  • Directly participate in Bidding for patient treatment cost.

 

E-Hospital View

  • Hospitals configure appointments for visiting doctors where patients can have direct access to appointment scheduler.
  • Hospital staff continuously in touch with doctor on updating patient health conditions.
  • Directly participate in Bidding for patient treatment cost.

 

Reports

  • Advanced patient centric reports based upon health data available.

 

E-Distributors View

  • Health care distributors promote their products to Doctors, Patients, Pharmacy shops, Diagnostic Lab’s.

 

Billing

  • Patient and treatment billing details.

 

Patient Medicine Remainders

  • Voice remainder for patient as per dosage plans.
