Fresh tender coconuts coming direct from Karnataka are available to the whole country. Our young natuarally grown coconuts are hand selected from coconut palms, giving the customer the freshest and most delicious coconuts possible. With average of 30 tons of coconuts exported per day, you are guaranteed fresh tender coconuts.
